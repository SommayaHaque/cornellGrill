FAQs

What is the Cornell Grilling Club?

The Cornell Grilling Club is a student-run organization dedicated to promoting the art of grilling and BBQ through community, education, and delicious food.

Who can join the club?

Anyone who is a student or affiliated with Cornell University can join the club, regardless of experience level or background.

Do I need to have prior grilling experience to join the club?

No, prior grilling experience is not required to join the club. We welcome members of all skill levels and are happy to teach newcomers how to grill.

How often does the club hold events?

The club typically holds events on a monthly basis, with additional events planned for holidays and special occasions.

Are there any membership fees?

Yes, there is a small fee for membership which helps to cover the cost of food and supplies for events.

What kind of events does the club host?

The club hosts a variety of events, including grilling competitions, educational workshops, social events, and community service projects.

Can I bring guests to club events?

Yes, guests are welcome at most club events. However, please check with the club leadership beforehand to make sure there is enough space and food for everyone.
