Joining

Welcome to the Cornell Grilling Club

We are a community of grill enthusiasts who love to share our passion for grilling with others. Our club hosts a variety of events and activities throughout the year, from cookouts to competitions and more. Check out our social media handles to stay up-to-date with the latest news and upcoming events.

Club Events :

May Cookout:

Join us for our annual Cookout!. we'll be grilling up delicious burgers, hot dogs, and vegetarian options. This event is open to all members and is a great way to meet new people and enjoy some amazing food.

Iron Chef Competition:

Otherwise know as our grilling competition. Participants will be given a surprise ingredient to incorporate into their dished and a panel of judges will determine the winner

Sustainble Grilling Workshop:

Learning about sustainable grilling practices and safety precautions. This educational workshop will cover topics such as using eco-friendly fuels, reducing food waste, and choosing properly sourced meats.

End of Semester Tailgate:

Join us for our annual Tailgate where we'll be grilling up some game-day favorites. This event is open to everyone and is a great way to show your school spirit and enjoy some delicious food before the big game between rival frats.

Club Mission

At the Cornell Grilling Club, our mission is to promote a love of grilling, safe practices and outdoor community. We believe that grilling is about both the food and experience of connecting with others. We aim to make an inclusive environment that welcomes all backgrounds and levels of experience to make the college experience more enjoyable

Club Values

The Cornell Grilling Club is committed to practicing responsible grilling and prioritzes sustainability. Promoting safety, while also valuing inclusivity and diversity, provides opportunitres for members to develop their grilling expertise. Besides prioritizing fun moments and the spirit of unity, we acknowledge that outdoor cooking creates meaningful memories when shared with those around us
